# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment

Arni is committed to providing a welcoming and productive environment for everyone, regardless of experience level, background, or identity.

## Expected Behaviour

- Be respectful and constructive in all interactions
- Give and receive feedback graciously
- Focus on what is best for the project and community
- Credit others for their ideas and contributions

## Unacceptable Behaviour

Disrespectful, exclusionary, or otherwise harmful conduct is not tolerated in any project space (issues, pull requests, discussions, or community channels).

## Enforcement

Maintainers have the right to remove, edit, or reject contributions and to temporarily or permanently ban contributors whose behaviour is harmful to the community.

## Reporting

To report a concern, contact the maintainers privately via GitHub. All reports will be handled with discretion.

---

This project adopts the spirit of the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).
